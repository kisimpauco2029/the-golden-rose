History of House Tyrell

House Tyrell of Highgarden is one of the Great Houses of Westeros, ruling over the fertile lands of the Reach. Their sigil is a golden rose on a green field, and their words are Growing Strong. Though not of ancient lineage, the Tyrells rose to prominence after the Targaryen conquest, when the last King of House Gardener perished at the Field of Fire. The Targaryens rewarded the Tyrells, stewards of Highgarden, with lordship over the Reach.

From that moment, the Tyrells became Wardens of the South, boasting great wealth, abundant harvests, and one of the largest armies in the realm. Known for their charm, chivalry, and political cunning, the family has often secured power through clever marriages and alliances.
