Bookworm with a sword. Quietly brilliant, always reading.

Appearance

Tall and dignified, Willas walks with a pronounced limp from a youthful jousting injury. He prefers scholarly robes or simple noble attire in muted greens and browns.

Lore & History

Willas is the eldest son of Mace Tyrell and Alerie Tyrell. Brother to Garlan, Loras, and Margaery. He is heir to Highgarden and excels in diplomacy, strategy, and scholarship.

Personality

Kind, thoughtful, and perceptive, Willas is admired across the Reach for his fair-mindedness and intelligence. Unlike his more flamboyant siblings, he values humility and learning.

Fun Facts / Achievements

  • Skilled breeder and trainer of dogs and hawks.
  • Known for his love of books and philosophy.
