Appearance

Broad-shouldered and knightly, Garlan cuts a striking figure in armor, often adorned with roses worked into the design. Off the battlefield, he dresses plainly compared to his siblings.

Lore & History

Nicknamed “Garlan the Gallant,” he is famed for his martial skill and unwavering honor. Son of Mace and Alerie, brother to Willas, Loras, and Margaery.

Personality

Chivalrous, dependable, and humble, Garlan is devoted to his family. He acts as a guiding older brother to Loras and a protector to Margaery. He is also deeply devoted to his wife, Leonette, and often seeks to balance his knightly duties with his home life.

Fun Facts / Achievements

  • Renowned swordsman, considered even more skilled than Loras.
  • Known for his humility despite great acclaim.
