Appearance

Stocky and somewhat portly, Mace has a round face and a jovial demeanor. Often seen in richly embroidered garments reflecting the green and gold of House Tyrell.

Lore & History

Lord Mace Tyrell is the head of House Tyrell and Warden of the South. Married to Alerie Tyrell, he is father to Willas, Garlan, Loras, and Margaery. He oversees the Reach from Highgarden, a region famed for its fertility and wealth.

Personality

Affable, ceremonial, and devoted to his family. Though sometimes considered bumbling, he genuinely cares for his people and seeks to uphold the prominence of House Tyrell.

Fun Facts / Achievements

  • Loves hosting lavish tournaments and celebrations.
  • Known for generosity, though sometimes misjudged in political matters.
  • Maintains House Tyrell’s wealth and alliances successfully despite occasional missteps.
