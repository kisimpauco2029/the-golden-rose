Queen of elegance and calm. Knows how to keep this house in check.

Appearance

Graceful and poised, Alerie carries the refinement of her Hightower lineage. She favors flowing gowns of white and pale green, often accented with pearls and silver.

Lore & History

Born of House Hightower, Alerie married into House Tyrell, strengthening the bonds between two of the Reach’s most powerful families. As wife to Mace Tyrell, she is mother to Willas, Garlan, Loras, and Margaery.

Personality

Measured and intelligent, Alerie balances Mace’s bluster with calm practicality. She is dedicated to her children, ensuring their education, refinement, and alliances reflect the prestige of their house.

Fun Facts / Achievements

  • Acts as a steady influence within House Tyrell.
  • Highly regarded for her keen organizational skills and sharp political instincts.
