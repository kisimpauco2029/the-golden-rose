Appearance

Elderly yet dignified, Olenna dresses in practical gowns with rich fabrics, favoring darker shades with subtle embroidery. She is rarely seen without her headdress, framing her sharp, witty gaze.

Lore & History

Known as the “Queen of Thorns,” Olenna hails from House Redwyne before her marriage into the Tyrells. She wielded considerable influence in both Highgarden and King’s Landing, often steering her family’s political course with unmatched cunning.

Personality

Quick-witted, razor-tongued, and unafraid to speak her mind, Olenna is the matriarch who ensures the Tyrells maintain their power. Though blunt, her wisdom is rooted in care for her family’s survival.

Fun Facts / Achievements

  • Master strategist behind many Tyrell alliances.
  • Famously orchestrated pivotal political moves in King’s Landing.
