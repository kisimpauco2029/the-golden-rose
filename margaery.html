Appearance

Beautiful and elegant, Margaery favors fashionable gowns in green and gold. Her bright smile and sparkling eyes draw attention wherever she goes.

Lore & History

The youngest Tyrell, Margaery is daughter of Mace and Alerie. Sister to Willas, Garlan, and Loras. She rose to prominence through her charm, wit, and strategic marriages, becoming Queen of the Seven Kingdoms.

Personality

Charming, clever, and compassionate, Margaery is skilled at winning the hearts of nobles and smallfolk alike. She shares a close, playful bond with Loras, filled with teasing, encouragement, and a deep trust that comes from growing up together in Highgarden.
